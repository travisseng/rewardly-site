Rewardly Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

Rewardly does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All user data — including habit progress and reward settings — is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

If you contact us for support, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to share. This information will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties.

If our privacy practices change, we will update this policy and reflect the changes on this page.

For questions or concerns, please contact us at support@rewardlyapp.com.
